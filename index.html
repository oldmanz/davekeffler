Thank you and Happy New Year!

First, thank you to everyone who has helped with an end of the year gift! These gifts help my ministry reach students on campus at WMU and beyond. As I look ahead to 2025 I'm excited to see what God is doing at H2O and in Collegiate Church Network. We've already got many things planned including a winter retreat on Jan 9-10th in Ann Arbor, The Well, Spring break, and LT to name a few.
